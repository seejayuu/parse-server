Community Guidelines

Our goal is to connect the world's people, places and products, whether it's online or offline. And since Poppo is a community, we want our users to act like part of a community. In short, that means treating people – and the service – with respect. So, here's a short list of do's and don'ts to guide your actions on Poppo.

 

DON'T

  • Don't be a jerk

  • Don't spam

  • Don't share content that's too racy, gory or otherwise offensive

  • Don't pretend to be someone else

  • Don't harass or threaten people

  • Don't break any laws or use Poppo to plan such things

  • Don't post hateful content

  • Don't violate copyrights

 

Please remember that these community guidelines help us ensure that the Member Content published is relevant, respectful, and useful.  While we appreciate your participation, we reserve the right to remove Member Content at any time for any reason or delete your account with or without warning.

 

DO

  • Do help us maintain a safe, engaging, and high quality community

  • Do share authentic content that relate your first‐hand experiences

  • Do join the fun and have a good time

  • Do be yourself and respectful of others

  • Do add value with interesting, informative and useful content

  • Do empower others to take action and help people shine

  • Do invite your friends and share what's important to you

But most of all enjoy yourself.  Poppo was built for you and is your community. We want you to interact and share your moments and interests with others.  We hope you'll find something new to love as you get to know other Poppo people and the community. Each and every person on Poppo makes the product what it is, so happy popping!

- Team Poppo
